Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Openfore collects information you provide directly, including account registration details, profile information, handicap, playing preferences, and location data. We may also collect usage data through cookies, device identifiers, and analytics technologies in order to improve the performance and functionality of our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the Openfore platform. This includes connecting you with other golfers, personalizing your experience, sending notifications related to tee times and invitations, maintaining account functionality, and complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Information Sharing

Your public profile information may be visible to other Openfore users as part of the social features of the platform. We do not sell your personal information. We may share aggregated or anonymized data with partners to improve our services. Information may also be disclosed when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users and platform.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information. However, no internet-based system can guarantee complete security, and we cannot ensure or warrant the absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. You can update your information through your account settings within the app or contact us directly to request changes or deletion of your data.
