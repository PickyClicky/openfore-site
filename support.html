Common Questions

How do I create a tee time?
Open the app and tap "Create Tee Time" to add your round and invite players.

How do I join a round?
Browse available rounds in the "Find a Round" section and tap "Join".

Is Openfore free?
Yes. Openfore is free to download and use.
