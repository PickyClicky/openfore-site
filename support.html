1. What is Openfore?

Openfore is a golf app that helps golfers share tee times, fill open spots, find rounds, and coordinate with other players more easily.

2. How do I download Openfore?

Download Openfore from the Apple App Store on your iPhone, then open the app and create or sign into your account.

3. How do I create an account?

Open the app, choose the sign-up option, complete the required account details, and follow the prompts to finish setup and access the app.

4. Do I need to be 18 or older to use Openfore?

Yes. Openfore is intended for adults only. Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account, access the app, and communicate with other users.

5. I can’t sign in. What should I do?

First, make sure you are using the correct email and password. If the problem continues, close and reopen the app, confirm your connection is working, and then contact support if you still cannot access your account.

6. I didn’t receive my verification email. What should I check?

Check your junk or spam folder, make sure you entered the correct email address, and wait a few minutes. If it still does not arrive, contact support and we will help you troubleshoot.

7. What if I forgot my password?

Use the password reset option in the app if it is available. If you are still unable to regain access, email support for help with your account.

8. How do I create a tee time?

In the app, go to the area for creating or posting a round, enter the course and tee time details, and save or publish it so other golfers can view it.

9. How do I join a tee time?

Browse available rounds, open the tee time you want, and tap the join option. The exact flow may depend on the tee time settings and whether approval is required.

10. Can I cancel or leave a tee time after joining?

If that option is available in the app, open the relevant tee time and follow the prompts to leave or cancel. If you are having trouble, contact support with the tee time details.

11. Can I edit a tee time after I create it?

If editing is enabled for your posted tee time, open the round and update the details from the available options. If you do not see the correct controls, make sure the app is up to date.

12. Can I delete a tee time I created?

If the app provides a delete or remove option for rounds you created, use that from the relevant tee time screen. If something appears stuck or incorrect, send support the details.

13. Why isn’t chat or messaging working?

Make sure you have an internet connection, the app is current, and you are signed into the correct account. If messages are not loading or sending, close and reopen the app, then contact support if the issue continues.

14. Why am I not receiving notifications?

Check that notifications are enabled for Openfore in your iPhone settings and that your device is not blocking alerts. If notifications still do not arrive, update the app and test again.

15. How do I update my profile?

Open your profile in the app and use the available edit options to update information such as your details, preferences, or profile image where supported.

16. Can I change the email on my account?

If email changes are supported in the app, update it from your profile or account settings. If not, contact support and include your current email and the email you want associated with your account.

17. How do I request deletion of my account and data?

If Openfore provides an in-app delete account option, you can use that directly. You can also contact support at support@openfore.golf and request account deletion assistance.

18. How do I report a user or a safety concern?

If a report feature is available in the app, use it directly from the relevant profile or interaction. For urgent support or to provide additional details, contact support and include as much information as possible.

19. Can I block another user?

If blocking is available in the app, use the option from the relevant user profile or menu. If you believe someone is misusing the platform, report the issue to support as well.

20. Where can I find the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy?

You can access the legal pages on the Openfore website. These pages explain the rules, privacy practices, and conditions that apply to your use of the app.

21. What should I do if the app crashes or freezes?

Close the app fully and reopen it. Make sure your iPhone has the latest iOS updates available and that you are running the latest version of Openfore. If the problem continues, email support with the steps that triggered the issue.

22. Which devices does Openfore support?

Openfore is designed for supported iPhone devices running a compatible version of iOS available through the App Store listing.

23. Is Openfore free to use?

Yes. Openfore is free to download and use unless otherwise stated in the app or App Store listing.

24. Why might the app ask for permissions like notifications or location?

Permissions may be used to improve the app experience, such as helping with alerts or relevant golf-related functionality. You can manage app permissions in your iPhone settings.

25. How do I contact Openfore support?

Email support@openfore.golf with your question or issue. Include your device model, iOS version, and a clear description of what happened so we can help more efficiently.
