Openfore is designed to help golfers connect and coordinate tee times. This policy protects the platform and its users from abuse, fraud, and technical attacks.

Users may not use the Service to engage in any activity that is unlawful, harmful, abusive, disruptive, or deceptive.

Prohibited activities include but are not limited to:

  • Fraud, scams, or deceptive practices

  • Using the Service for illegal activities

Users may not use the platform to send spam or unsolicited messages.

Automated access to the Service is prohibited without written permission.

Users must not exploit platform features in ways that harm the Service or its users.
